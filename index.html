Shipping

All shipments are insured and all parts are covered by our personal guarantee.

From the smallest service part to a full restoration we are able to assist. Our shipments come via airfreight courier for rapid service.

Please note that we are not a retail shop. Our business is based online, email and phone.

Parts Catalogues

We keep stocks of service and repair items for these cars and import non stock items from the overseas suppliers.

We only deal in new parts from authorised British Motor Heritage approved suppliers. We do not sell 2nd hand parts.

On request we will source parts for other makes where possible.
